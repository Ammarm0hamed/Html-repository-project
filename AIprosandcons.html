Artificial intelligence can be used in mining or other process of exploration. The machines used in this area, as well as the robots, are so complex that they can be used for exploring the ocean floor. The intelligent robots also can be sent to explore space, because they are made with metal bodies and are more resistant to maintain in the space. Also, there are these new, driverless cars that could replace millions of human drivers, such as taxi or bus drivers.
The smartphones are a simple example of how we use AI in our everyday life. We are travelling with GPS, using the virtual assistants such as Siri, taking pictures and detecting the person’s face, etc. The AI is much expensive.
